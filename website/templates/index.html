About Zpredict

Zpredict is your personalized academic guide, built for Sri Lankan students. We combine AI, machine learning, and official UGC datasets to help you make smarter choices about your university and career path. From predicting cutoff marks to suggesting careers aligned with your strengths, we are here to make your higher education journey less stressful and more empowering.

Whether you are unsure about which university program suits you best or need clarity on aptitude tests, our platform provides insights, recommendations, and chatbot support.We built Zpredict to give you the tools we wish we had.
